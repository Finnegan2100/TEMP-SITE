Hi, I'm Ben. Before switching to HTML5 I worked on a number of games in Flash. I've given talks on HTML5 Game development at Microsoft, written tutorials for Intel and my latest position was HTML5 Game Developer and Technical Coordinator at Sesame Street. I live in New York City and enjoy piano and running.
